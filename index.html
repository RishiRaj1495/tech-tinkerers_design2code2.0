Biography — About Me

Hello! I'm Rishi Raj.

I am currently pursuing my BTech degree at VIT Bhopal University. I serve as a Core Member of the UX Club at VIT Bhopal, where I work with a group of passionate designers and programmers. We aim to promote user-centered design paradigms and bridge the gap between design theory and technical implementation.

My approach focuses on simplicity, clear visual hierarchy, and developer-friendly design assets. I believe that digital interfaces should be effortless to use and visually pleasing. That is why I design in Figma, and build using modern front-end technologies like React, custom CSS, and responsive HTML frameworks.

I am actively looking for design challenges, internships, and collaborations where I can contribute my skillsets in interaction layout design and product development. Let's make things that matter!
